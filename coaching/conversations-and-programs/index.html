What I’d like you to know before choosing to work with me

I feel it’s important that as you consider entering into a coaching relationship, you are provided with information that helps you understand what coaching involves, what it asks of you and what it ‘looks’ like.

Are you willing to ‘take a look’ and to do things that are outside your norm?  This is where new seeing and new development takes place.

I am offering to work with you on a ‘topic’ that deeply matters to you; one that you’ve likely had difficulty making progress in and/or sustaining progress in. I am also offering an approach to change that focuses on the development of competencies in you that enable you to be in your coaching topic in a new way and achieve new results.

When we work together I invite you to bring both your personal and your professional self as we explore your coaching topic. I will design a customized Coaching Program for you, one that is anchored in your coaching topic, is tailored to meet you right where you are and enables you to make meaningful progress on your topic.

In between coaching meetings you can expect that you will need to actively engage in daily exercises and practices that are custom designed for you and the focus of your development in your Coaching Program. I will encourage, support and challenge you to take a look at yourself and to try ‘different’ as part of your development.

The first exploratory meeting is free and allows us both an opportunity to see whether there is a good ‘fit’. If you’re interested in coaching have a look at the options available to you.

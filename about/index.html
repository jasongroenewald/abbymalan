As a Certified Integral Master Coach™, I draw from a rich tapestry of methodologies to develop a customized approach for each unique client.

With formal qualifications in psychology, mental health, yoga and business I have, over the past 17 years, focused my professional development in Coaching and Facilitation and have studied with both Integral Coaching Canada and the Tavistock Consultancy in the UK.

Examples of current and recent clients include Johnson and Johnson, Investec Private Bank, Coronation Fund Managers, British American Tobacco, Red Cross Children’s Hospital, Old Mutual, South African College of Applied Psychology (SACAP), The Clothing Bank, CARE International, and Tourvest Travel Services. As an executive coach on ASCEND, Johnson & Johnson International’s Executive Development Programme for women, I coached women from Europe, the Middle East and Africa from 2009-2011. Since 2012 I have been an executive coach on the Women in Leadership Programme at the UCT Graduate School of Business and on a number of Leadership and Executive Development Programmes at Old Mutual. For both programmes I have coached executives from across Africa. From 2011 to 2015 I was one of 20 coaches to coach women on a two year Enterprise Development Programme at The Clothing Bank in Cape Town, South Africa with the mission to “empower unemployed mothers through enterprise development so that they can become financially and socially independent”. I have been a faculty member with Integral Coaching Canada since November 2014 and work globally with students of Integral Coaching. I have recently joined the team at 57 Coaches, a pro bono coaching initiative available to leaders within NGOs supporting Syrian refugees.

My preference is to coach individuals in leadership roles who are at a point in their career and personal life where they are asking questions of themselves that can be addressed in a coaching relationship. I am inspired by the growth and change I facilitate in individuals. My clients respect me for creating a safe and open space that enables them to achieve outcomes that deeply matter.

Rooted in my own commitment to living with personal integrity and authenticity, as a coach my aim is to enable people to grow and advance the health, meaning and integrity of their lives, in and outside the workplace.

I am originally from the West Coast of Canada and have been a permanent South African resident living in Cape Town since November 2001. I am a mother of two young daughters and am married to a South African. In my free time I enjoy trail running and yoga, reading and writing and exploring new places. My intention is to, as much as possible, maintain a healthy balance in my life.

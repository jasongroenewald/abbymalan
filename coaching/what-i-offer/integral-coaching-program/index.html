Integral Coaching® Program

If you feel ready to embark on making a significant impact on something that really matters to you and are committed to change, trying ‘new’, reflecting on past, current and potential new ways of being, and if you have the time to commit to a longer term coaching program then the Integral Coaching® program is for you.

Intake

We meet for 1.5-2 hours and I come to understand what your coaching topic looks like today and what it will look and feel like once it is attained. I get to learn about who you are, how you make sense of your topic and much more.

Offer

We meet for 1.5 hours and I offer to you your coaching program including insight into your current way of approaching your topic, a new way of moving forward in your topic and then some necessary steps to take in service of taking action on your topic.

Cycles of Development

We meet for 1 to 1.5 hours every 2-3 weeks and in between meetings you engage with exercises and practices specific to you making progress in your coaching topic. The purpose of these meetings is to facilitate and support you through the stretches (the highs and the lows) of your development as you build new muscles that will expand how you are able to be in your topic in a way that supports fulfillment of your topic.

Completion

We meet for 1.5 hours to recognize and acknowledge the journey we’ve been on, the progress you have made and what is now possible beyond the coaching program.
 
Integral Coaching® programs usually last from 5-12 months, depending on the scope of your topic.

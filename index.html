About me

I coach to make progress on topics that matter deeply to you. I will have served you well if you leave each session feeling challenged yet firmly held. If you breathe a little easier then much has been accomplished. On the path we agree to take together you will experience tastes of a new way and of new possibilities. To serve you most fully I meet you right where you are at in your life, wherever that may be. In the process we honor you for who and how you have been up until now.

Learn more about me

Not only did I feel heard, but I really felt understood. Euphoric is the word that comes to mind when I think of my time with Abby. Managing Director, The South African College of Applied Psychology

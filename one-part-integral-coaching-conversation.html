If you want to have a conversation that illuminates some new awareness’s and gives you a feel for the coaching experience then you might consider a one-part conversation which includes the following:

We will meet for 2 hours during which time I come to understand what your coaching topic looks like. I get to learn about who you are, how you make sense of your topic and what you’ve tried up until now to make progress in this topic. We decide on the important steps that are needed to make some progress and we discuss possible exercises and practices to support you. You will receive a follow up email with an outline of your coaching topic and goals as well as 1-2 practices for you to embark on, on your own.
